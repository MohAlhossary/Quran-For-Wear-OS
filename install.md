# Quran For Wear OS Installaton Guide

Our application is not yet public on the Google Play Store, so to get it right now, you have to join to be a beta tester. Here is how:

## Joining the Beta Testers list
- Join the Google Group by going to [https://groups.google.com/g/quran-for-wear-os](https://groups.google.com/g/quran-for-wear-os) and clicking the 'Join group' button.
<img width="490" height="249" alt="Join group Button" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/770a559e-c376-408e-8d27-ad7f4b175515" /> </br>
- Then, click 'Join group' on the popup, and you are officially invited to the beta testers program.
<img width="210" height="210" alt="Join group Popup" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/267ff9f3-9ec3-44e4-b2b6-542d64402db1" /> </br>
- Next, go to [https://play.google.com/apps/testing/app.quran4wearos](https://play.google.com/apps/testing/app.quran4wearos), and join the beta testers program.
<img width="405" height="250" alt="Beta testers screen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6f975557-5702-45f3-935e-5f2196ee7081" /> </br>
- If you see this screen, you did not join the Google Group and have to redo this section again, if not, you can move on
<img width="450" height="250" alt="Error joining the Google Group" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bffbc600-2002-4328-b426-458f99890071" /> </br>

## Downloading the app
- Now, click 'download it on Google Play'. </br>
<img width="383" height="250" alt="Beta Testers Program" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/641fc979-ef5d-4603-9259-54c80a8993dd" /> </br>
- After that, click install on more devices'. </br>
<img width="426" height="240" alt="Installing the app" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8507bcc1-320c-4f65-a70c-ed13218ec16a" /> </br>
- Choose your desired device, and click install </br>
<img width="426" height="240" alt="Install popup" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/654e9e7e-3e57-4df0-a032-6388bb58964d" /> </br>
- Now, Click OK </br>
<img width="426" height="240" alt="Install confirmation popup" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f93fe4b-85ab-4fd4-a99e-f04ffd893c5e" /> </br>
