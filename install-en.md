# Quran For Wear OS Installaton Guide

Our application is not yet public on the Google Play Store, so to get it right now, you have to join the beta testing program and become a beta tester.

That is done on 2 steps:

## 1) Joining the Beta Testers Google group
- In your browser, go to the Google Group page here [https://groups.google.com/g/quran-for-wear-os](https://groups.google.com/g/quran-for-wear-os)
- Click the '**Join group**' button.
<img width="490" height="249" alt="Join group Button" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/770a559e-c376-408e-8d27-ad7f4b175515" />

- Then, click '**Join group**' on the popup, and you are officially _**invited**_ to the beta testers program.
<img width="210" height="210" alt="Join group Popup" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/267ff9f3-9ec3-44e4-b2b6-542d64402db1" />

## 2) Getting the app
### - Getting access to the app by actually becoming a tester
- Go to the Google play store [https://play.google.com/apps/testing/app.quran4wearos](https://play.google.com/apps/testing/app.quran4wearos), 
- If you see this screen, that means you did not join the Google Group, or you joined it using a different Google account. In this case, you have make sure you _**joined**_ the Google group and you use the same Google account for both the Google group and the play store. otherwise, you can move on.
<img width="450" height="250" alt="Error joining the Google Group" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/bffbc600-2002-4328-b426-458f99890071" />

- Join the beta testers program by clicking **become a tester**.
<img width="405" height="250" alt="Beta testers screen" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6f975557-5702-45f3-935e-5f2196ee7081" />

- You should now see a screen saying you are a tester.
<img width="383" height="250" alt="Beta Testers Program" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/641fc979-ef5d-4603-9259-54c80a8993dd" />

### - Downloading the app
- Now, click 'download it on Google Play'.

- After that, click install on more devices'.
<img width="426" height="240" alt="Installing the app" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8507bcc1-320c-4f65-a70c-ed13218ec16a" />

- Choose your smartwatch, and click **install**
<img width="426" height="240" alt="Install popup" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/654e9e7e-3e57-4df0-a032-6388bb58964d" />

- Now, Click **OK**
<img width="426" height="240" alt="Install confirmation popup" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/2f93fe4b-85ab-4fd4-a99e-f04ffd893c5e" />
